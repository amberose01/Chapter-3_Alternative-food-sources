Metadata for treatment_res 
Treatment	the specific treatment to be graphed. Includes either control, wheast_paste, wheast_spray, fructose, or sprite
Plant	the specific plant ID given to each tree. Tree number 41 for each treatment represents the average for each treatment group.
Day	the day that data was collected. Data was collected 1 hour, 4 hours, 8 hours and 24 hours after predator release, with data collection occurring on a 24 hours interval after this period
Num_sdb	the number of scale destroyer beetles observed on each plant during each observation period (day).
